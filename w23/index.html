Coding Club

Achievements:

  • Organized 36-hour PICT CodeStorm Hackathon

Robotics Club

Description: Focuses on building innovative robots and participating in intercollegiate robotic competitions. Offers hands-on experience in IoT and embedded systems.

Achievements:

  • Finalists at IIT TechFest RoboWar
  • Built autonomous delivery robot prototype
  • Workshops on Arduino & Raspberry Pi

Dance Club (PICT Rhythm)

Description: A vibrant group of dancers across styles like hip-hop, classical, and contemporary. Known for energetic performances at college fests.

Achievements:

  • Winners of "Pulse" Cultural Fest 2023
  • Performed at Firodiya Karandak Finals
  • Workshops conducted by professional choreographers

Drama Club (PICT Rangmanch)

Description: A passionate group of actors, writers, and directors who perform stage plays, mimes, and street theatre to raise social awareness.

Achievements:

  • Winners of Firodiya Karandak 2024
  • Performed street plays on mental health awareness
  • Scriptwriting and acting workshops

Photography Club (Shutterbugs)

Description: Captures moments from campus life and events. Organizes photo walks and editing tutorials for budding photographers.

Achievements:

  • Photo of the Month contest winner on AICTE portal
  • Covered all major PICT events and cultural fests
  • Exhibition held during Impressions 2024
