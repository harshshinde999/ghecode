Movie Reviews

Inception

Genre: Sci-Fi, Action

Summary: A thief who steals corporate secrets through dream-sharing is offered a chance to erase his past crimes.

Review: A mind-bending masterpiece by Christopher Nolan. Excellent performances and breathtaking visuals make this a must-watch.

The Dark Knight

Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

Summary: Batman faces off against the Joker, a criminal mastermind causing chaos in Gotham City.

Review: An iconic superhero film with a legendary performance by Heath Ledger. Intense and gripping throughout.

Interstellar

Genre: Sci-Fi, Adventure, Drama

Summary: A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in search of a new habitable planet.

Review: A visually stunning and emotionally resonant film that explores space, time, and love. Hans Zimmer’s score is unforgettable.

Parasite

Genre: Thriller, Drama

Summary: A poor family schemes to infiltrate a wealthy household by posing as unrelated, highly qualified individuals.

Review: A darkly humorous and socially critical masterpiece that keeps you on edge. Brilliant storytelling and direction.

3 Idiots

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Summary: Three engineering students explore friendship and pressure in the Indian education system.

Review: A heartwarming, funny, and thought-provoking film that resonates deeply with students and professionals alike.
